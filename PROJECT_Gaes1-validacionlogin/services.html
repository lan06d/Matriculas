Maintenance

Air conditioners, like all electronic equipment, need regular maintenance to extend its useful life. Air conditioning maintenance is divided into three types:

Types of maintenance

They are maintenance tasks that are executed to prevent, reduce risks and prolong the life of equipment useful. It includes tasks such as keeping the air conditioner clean and lubricated, checking and keep the parts of the indoor unit such as screws, electric terminals and so on to prevent failures or breakdowns in the operation of the equipment.

It consists of collecting data on the operation of the air conditioning to predict when the equipment will require the next maintenance. The technical team must monitor and keep a constant record of the normal values of pressure, temperature, power consumption, amperage and voltage that should be have air conditioning. This makes it possible to identify when anomalous values are present for plan a revision or replacement of some component before another failure occurs complex.
